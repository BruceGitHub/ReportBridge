# Choose Your Scaffold

Use this matrix to pick the right scaffold and starting maturity level.

## Decision Matrix

| Situation | Recommended Scaffold | Start Level | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Product SaaS with regular releases | saas-product | standard | Balance between speed and technical sustainability |
| Agency / software house with many client projects | software-house | lite | Fast setup and low overhead |
| Internal enterprise platform/team | enterprise-internal | standard | Needs governance, traceability, and handover quality |
| Early startup, small team, high uncertainty | startup-early-stage | lite | Minimal process, quick execution |

## Maturity Levels

- lite: only essential docs (quick onboarding, key decisions, recurring issues)
- standard: adds technical standards, operational procedures, and periodic review
- advanced: adds full governance, richer traceability, and formal quality loops
